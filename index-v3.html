The Coach

A player-first method, refined over three decades.

Jeff Zipman has spent thirty years on the court — not just playing, but studying every angle of the game. As a Tennis Canada Certified Coach 1, he brings a methodical, player-first approach that adapts to your level, your goals, and the specific way you move.

Whether you are picking up a racket for the first time or sharpening a competitive edge, the work begins the same way: observe, record, diagnose, adjust. Then run it back. The goal is never the session — it is the next ten.

“See what the eye misses. Fix what the body hides.”

— Coaching philosophy, in one line.

Sessions blend technical precision — grip, stance, point of contact — with the kind of court intuition that only comes from decades of dedicated practice. The outcome: players who leave each lesson knowing exactly what to drill before the next one.
